How to Use the Pomodoro Technique

By breaking your work into manageable intervals, you can stay motivated and productive throughout your study sessions.

  1. Set a timer for 25 minutes and focus on a single task
  2. Take a 5-minute break after each Pomodoro
  3. After four Pomodoros, take a longer break of 30 minutes
  4. Repeat the cycle to maintain focus and productivity
  5. By breaking your work into manageable intervals, you can stay motivated and productive throughout your study sessions.
